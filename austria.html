Austria has recently completely remodelled its work visa system and has introduced the Red-White-Red Card, which grants legal permanent residence.


Red-White-Red Card

Austria has introduced new criteria based immigration scheme: the Red-White-Red Card (RWR Card).  The RWR Card model aims at a more efficient immigration system for the qualified third-country workforce and their families who wish to settle permanently in Austria according to personal and labor market criteria.

The most important requirements are the qualification, work experience, age, language skills, an offer of employment according to the qualification and minimum remuneration.

         Types of RWR Card

The RWR Card is available for people who meet the requirements in two different versions:
1) RWR Card: entitles to residence and employment only with a certain employer
2) RWR Card plus: entitles to the residence and unlimited labour market access

This visa is issued for a time of a year. A six month’s living arrangement permit is given to the equipped jobseekers to seek after their employment. This Red-White-Red Visa enables the cardholder to bring his/her life partner and kids underneath the age of 18. The RWR Card holder's life partner can secure a RWR Card in addition, which will allow him/her to work in Austria.

EU Blue Card

Austria EU Blue Card is introduced as an immigration card as par with US Green Card. This will allow highly educated skilled professionals from foreign locations to come to Austria and explore the opportunities available in this beautiful rich country.

It is issued in the shape of a bank card. This is a combination of residence permit and work permit. Austria EU Blue Card entitles the visa holder for temporary settlement and employment with a certain employer. The card has a validity period of 2 years.

As a third-country citizen one may be granted an EU Blue Card if they fulfil all the details required for attaining the card.

Austria Blue Card Advantages

         Holders of Austria EU Blue Card are capable of applying for an Austria RWR card after completion of at least 21 months of employment in Austria in their 24 months stay.
         Immediate family unification.
         Unobstructed labor market admittance to the cardholder and their family members
         Trail to Austrian PR
